Bazaar Setu

Bazaar Setu is a mobile application that allows users to search for shops, sellers, and businesses, view customer reviews, and connect with shopkeepers or service providers. The app enables customers to browse and purchase products through the Explore tab, while businesses can expand their reach and grow without any cost.

Overview

Bazaar Setu bridges the gap between businesses and customers, allowing shops and service providers to create a profile, showcase products, and interact with buyers. The platform supports retail, wholesale, manufacturing, and service industries.

Key Features

  • Business Discovery - Find shops, sellers, and service providers easily.
  • Customer Reviews & Ratings - Make informed decisions based on reviews.
  • Direct Contact & Messaging - Communicate with sellers directly.
  • Product Browsing & Purchasing - Explore and buy products easily.
  • Free Business Growth Tools - Expand your business at no cost.

Global Availability

Bazaar Setu is available worldwide. Its headquarters is based in Jaipur, India, but the platform is managed globally with offices in the USA, Dubai, and other locations.
